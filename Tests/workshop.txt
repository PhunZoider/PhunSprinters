description=[b]PhunSprinters 2[/b] the sequel to the Academy Award winning [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3532685233]PhunSprinters[/url].
description=
description=PhunSprinters is the ultimate sprinter mod for Zomboid. PhunSprinters does [b]not[/b] simply add sprinters.
description=It calculates [b]risk[/b] and that risk can change depending on [b]where you are, how long you’ve survived, the moon cycle, and the environment[/b].
description=
description=[h1]🧠 How Sprinter Risk Is Calculated[/h1]
description=[hr]
description=Sprinter behaviour is determined by stacking modifiers in this order:
description=[list]
description=[*]Global Base Risk (in your settings)
description=[*]Zone Override (if PhunZones 2 is installed)
description=[*]Hours Discount (Ease-In)
description=[*]Moon Phase Modifier
description=[*]Darkness / Night Rules
description=[*]Light Suppression
description=[*]Final Sprinter Chance & Behaviour
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h1]🌑 Modes[/h1]
description=[hr]
description=There are four different modes that determine when sprinters will run
description=[table]
description=[tr][th]Mode[/th][th]Description[/th][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Always [/b][/td][td]Sprinters will always run[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]Dusk till Dawn[/b][/td][td]Sprinters will walk during daytime, but when the sun goes down, they run[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]When its too dark outside[/b][/td][td]During the night or when weather reduces visibility, zeds will run (even if its daytime).[/td][/tr]
description=[tr][td][b]When its too dark around them[/b][/td][td]This is the phunest option, but least performant. In this mode, sprinters will run if it ever gets too dark around them. In this mode, your flashlight becomes a critical part of gameplay.[/td][/tr]
description=[/table]
description=
description=[h1]🔦 Light Suppression (Flashlights Matter)[/h1]
description=[hr]
description=When enabled, bright light can force sprinters to walk
description=
description=This creates emergent gameplay:
description=[list]
description=[*]Flashlights become tactical tools
description=[*]Base lighting matters
description=[*]Night travel becomes a risk vs reward decision
description=[*]It brings some of the Phun of mode #4 to the other options (this option doesn't do anything if using Mode 4)
description=[/list]
description=
description=⚠️ Some limits exist to prevent performance issues in dense areas. 
description=
description=[h1]⏳Hours Discount (Difficulty Easing)[/h1]
description=[hr]
description=The [b]Hours Discount[/b] slowly ramps difficulty over time.
description=Instead of applying full risk immediately, sprinter chance increases gradually.
description=
description=Example:
description=[list]
description=[*]Zone Risk: 50%
description=[*]Hours Discount: 100
description=[*]Player Hours: 50
description=[/list]
description=
description=➡️ Effective Risk = 25%
description=
description=Risk increases linearly until the discount hours are reached.
description=
description=This is ideal for fresh servers, new characters, and multiplayer balance.
description=
description=[h1]🌕 Moon Phase Modifiers[/h1]
description=[hr]
description=Moon phases [b]multiply[/b] the current risk.
description=
description=Example:
description=[list]
description=[*]Zone Risk: 20%
description=[*]Full Moon Modifier: 200 → will effectively double (200%) the risk to 40%
description=[*]New Moon Modifier: 50 → will effectively half (50%) the risk to 10%
description=[/list]
description=
description=Moon modifiers scale risk, they do not replace it. Unless you set it to 0, then it means there is no risk!
description=
description=Key differences to version 1
description=[list]
description=[*]Support for PhunZones 2

description=[*]Unified the UI and core mods (UI is no longer a separate mod)

description=[*]Improve performance
description=[*]Reduce amount of "Why they no sprint" moments
description=[/list]
